# Bayes信息准则

由[[后验概率的正态近似]]：
$$\begin{align}
\ln p(\mathcal{D}) & \approx -\mathcal{E}(\hat{\theta})-\frac12 \ln|\mathbf{H}|+ \frac{D}{2} \ln2\pi \\
&= -\ln p(\hat{\theta},\mathcal{D})-\frac12 \ln|\mathbf{H}|+ \frac{D}{2} \ln2\pi \\
&= -\ln p(\hat{\theta}),\mathcal{D}-\frac12 \ln|\mathbf{H}|+ \frac{D}{2} \ln2\pi
\end{align} $$
